    Facilities

    The New College Building "Aurobindo", is a well planned structure to provide the best infrastructure to the students. The open spaces, well ventilated class rooms and a modern impressive structure is an attraction point for students to spend more time in the premise.

    The Computer Lab

    With more than 120 computers, our Computer Laboratory is well equiped with latest hardware and software to suit the multi faculty requirements and the same is upgraded on a continuous bases to keep pace with the developments in the field.

    Sound Studio

    The professionally developed and designed studio provides the latest equipment and technology required to learn all aspects of sound, recording, editing, dubbing, mixing and film editing. The media lab is equipped with photography and handycams, Audio visual and public addressing system.

    Gymkhana

    A committee, consisting of teachers & students manage the activities of the gymkhana. The College Sports Department encourages interested students by allowing them to compete in various InterCollegiate Sports Tournament

    Hostel Facility

    Hostel facility is available for outstation students (both girls and boys)
