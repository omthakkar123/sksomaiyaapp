    Aim

    The college library aims to develop collection of traditional and new forms of information resources to enable members in their personal growth opportunities.

    Mission

    The library mission is to encourage creativity of members through achieving excellence in acquiring, organizing and disseminating information resources.

    About Us

    A centralized air conditioned library is spread across two floors over 8000 square feet. The library offers excellent collection of books, journals and other resources in literature, computer science, information technology, social sciences, humanities and management. Collection of reference, text, periodicals and audio and visual resources is maintained separately in the library

    Circulation

    Book circulation (issue and return) service is available on first floor of the library. A student can borrow two textbooks from the library for eight days. College ID card is used for book circulation therefore the student doesnâ€™t need to carry a separate library card to borrow books.

    Reprography

    Library offers photocopying facility to all members on charge of Rupees one per copy. In the library one can photocopy a maximum of 10 copies per day

    Book Bank

    It is an extended facility given to economically backward students. They can borrow textbooks for each semester / year against refundable deposit.
